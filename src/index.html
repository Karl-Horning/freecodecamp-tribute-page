Homer Simpson

A tribute to a husband, a father, and cultural icon.

Although Homer can be selfish, he’s never mean on purpose

Any time that Homer is selfish it stems from a lack of understanding - never from malice. Once Homer realises what he has done, he tries to make it right.

Homer never stops trying

Although Homer and Lisa are very different people, Homer never stops trying to bond with her. Homer also often tries to maintain a strong relationship with Bart. Homer always helps Bart whenever he needs it most.

Homer is a devoted husband

Throughout the years Homer has had several opportunities to be unfaithful. Despite this, he always does the right thing (usually after realising that he is being hit on).

Homer is loyal to his family

Homer has gone to great lengths to help and make his family happy. Homer has worked almost twenty-four hours a day to give Lisa a pony. Homer deal’s with Marge’s sisters who often undermine him. Homer has pretended to be Selma’s husband so that she could adopt a baby because Marge asked him to do it. Homer stood up to Goose Gladwell when Gladwell sold the rights to Bart’s t-shirt designs to Disney. There are many more examples.

Homer is a factotum

Despite his lack of intelligence, Homer has held over 190 jobs throughout the years. He was even a NASA astronaut!
